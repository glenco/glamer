Read the points in from a snapshot of the halos created by RockStar. The output will be the halos in the cone in coordinates where the x-axis is the center of the cone. Changing the observer, xo, and direction of view, V, effectively translates and rotates the box, respectively. Only halos with radii between rlow and rhigh are added.
